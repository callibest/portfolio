Solar Security Systems

Job done by Callistus Nwachukwu

Job Overview

Independently designed, built, and configured Solar Trailers, Solar Bastion, Hinged LID Bastion, MINI Bastion & Solar POD, integrating solar energy systems with advanced security components to deliver reliable, off-grid surveillance solutions. This work involved the use of solar panels, tracking systems, and Victron Energy components such as MPPT solar charge controllers and Orion smart DC-DC chargers to ensure efficient power management and system stability. Each unit was equipped with switches, audio amplifiers, NVRs, IP camera heads, and audio sounders, delivering real-time monitoring, recording, and deterrence capabilities. These custom-built systems were engineered for durability, mobility, and autonomous operation in remote and demanding environments.
